What is this blog?

This blog is my corner on the Internet. I love to write, to share my thoughts with the world, to have great conversations with people and enjoy life. I'm forever grateful for the Internet to give me a seamless way to do that. Here I share my thoughts, my learnings and anything else I think about writing. This blog doesn't have any other goals than being a public record of my thoughts, so expect it to have a similar personality to mine. So if you like what I write, share your thoughts with me at hey@aswinmohan.me, if you don't like it, fuck off, but still hit me up at my email.
